## How to Use

### For HR Administrators

#### View Overall Compliance
1. Navigate to **Documents & Policies**
2. Click **UAE Documents** tab
3. Review statistics cards at top
4. Scan compliance dashboard

#### Check Specific Employee
1. Use search bar to find employee
2. View row in employee status table
3. Check individual document statuses
4. Review overall compliance status

#### Filter Employees
**By Status:**
- Select "All Statuses" dropdown
- Choose: Compliant, Warning, or Critical

**By Department:**
- Select department dropdown
- Choose specific department

#### Send Reminders
**Individual:**
- Click reminder icon next to employee
- Notification sent to employee

**Bulk:**
- Click "Send Bulk Reminder" button
- Reminders sent to all employees with expiring/expired documents

#### Export Data
- Click "Download" button
- Exports current view to CSV/Excel
- Includes all visible columns and filters

### For Employee Self-Service

#### View My Documents
1. Login to Employee Portal
2. Navigate to **My Documents**
3. View all uploaded documents
4. Check expiry dates

#### Check Alerts
**Critical Alert (Red):**
- Displayed prominently at top
- Lists all expired documents
- "Renew Now" button for each

**Warning Alert (Orange):**
- Displayed below critical alerts
- Lists expiring soon documents
- "Renew" button for each

#### Renew Document
1. Click "Renew Now" or "Renew" button
2. Fill in renewal information:
   - New Issue Date
   - New Expiry Date
   - Document Number
   - Notes (optional)
3. Upload renewed document (PDF/Image)
4. Click "Save Renewal"
5. Await HR verification

## Document Upload Guidelines

### File Requirements
- **Formats**: PDF, JPG, PNG
- **Max Size**: 10MB per file
- **Quality**: Clear, readable scan
- **Pages**: Include both sides if applicable

### Best Practices
‚úÖ **DO:**
- Upload clear, high-quality scans
- Ensure all information is visible
- Include both sides of card documents
- Verify dates before uploading
- Update expiry dates accurately
- Add relevant notes

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Upload blurry or unclear images
- Cut off any document information
- Upload expired documents as new
- Forget to update expiry dates
- Compress images too much

### UAE Document Specific Tips

**Emirates ID:**
- Scan front and back
- Ensure barcode is visible
- Include in color for security features

**Residence Visa:**
- Include full passport page with visa stamp
- Ensure visa number is clear
- Capture all entry/exit stamps if relevant

**Labour Card:**
- Scan both sides
- Ensure MOHRE stamp is visible
- Include company details page

**Work Permit:**
- Include all pages
- Ensure signatures are visible
- Capture approval stamps

**Health Insurance:**
- Upload insurance card front/back
- Include policy number page
- Attach coverage details if available

## Renewal Process

### Step-by-Step Renewal

#### Step 1: Identification
- System identifies expiring/expired document
- Alert displayed to employee and HR
- Renewal button enabled

#### Step 2: Initiation
- Employee clicks "Renew" or "Renew Now"
- Renewal form opens
- Current document info displayed

#### Step 3: Information Entry
Employee provides:
- New issue date
- New expiry date
- Document number
- Additional notes

#### Step 4: Document Upload
- Upload renewed document scan
- Ensure file meets requirements
- Multiple files if needed

#### Step 5: Submission
- Review information
- Click "Save Renewal"
- Success notification displayed

#### Step 6: Verification (HR)
- HR reviews submission
- Verifies document authenticity
- Approves or requests changes
- Status updated to "Verified"

### Renewal Timeline
Recommended to start renewal:
- **Emirates ID**: 2-3 months before expiry
- **Residence Visa**: 3-4 months before expiry
- **Labour Card**: Automatically renewed with visa
- **Work Permit**: Automatically renewed with visa
- **Health Insurance**: 1 month before expiry

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What happens if my document expires?
**A:** Your document will be marked as "Expired" with a critical alert. You should renew it immediately to maintain employment compliance.

### Q: How far in advance should I renew?
**A:** Start the renewal process 2-3 months before expiry to allow time for processing.

### Q: Can I upload multiple documents at once?
**A:** Currently, documents are uploaded individually. Bulk upload feature is planned for future release.

### Q: What if I don't have a document yet?
**A:** Contact HR immediately. Some documents are legally required for employment in UAE.

### Q: How do I know when my documents are expiring?
**A:** The system automatically displays alerts 90 days before expiry. You'll see warnings on your dashboard.

### Q: Can I view my document history?
**A:** Yes, each document shows upload date, verification date, and renewal history.

### Q: What if my renewal is rejected?
**A:** HR will contact you with specific reasons. You can then upload corrected documents.

### Q: Are reminders sent automatically?
**A:** Currently, HR sends manual reminders. Automatic email reminders are planned for future release.
