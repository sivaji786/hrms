# HR Management System

A comprehensive Human Resources Management System built with React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS. This system provides a complete suite of tools for managing employees, attendance, payroll, recruitment, and more.

## üåü Features

### Core Modules

1. **Dashboard** - Real-time overview of HR metrics with location-based filtering
2. **Employee Management** - Complete employee lifecycle management
3. **Attendance & Time Tracking** - Clock in/out, calendar view, and analytics
4. **Leave Management** - Leave requests, approvals, and balance tracking
5. **Payroll Management** - Salary processing, payslips, and tax calculations
6. **Recruitment (ATS)** - Job postings, applicant tracking, and hiring pipeline
7. **Performance Management** - Reviews, goals, and feedback system
8. **Training & Development** - Training programs and employee development
9. **Asset Management** - Company asset allocation and tracking
10. **Expense & Travel Management** - Expense claims and travel requests
11. **Document & Policy Management** - Company documents and policy repository
12. **Shift Management** - Shift scheduling and roster management
13. **Ticketing System** - Internal support and helpdesk
14. **Notifications** - Real-time alerts and updates
15. **Admin & Role Management** - User roles and permissions
16. **Reports & Analytics** - Comprehensive reporting and insights
