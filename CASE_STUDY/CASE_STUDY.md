## üéØ Project Overview

### The Challenge

Small to medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in the UAE and Middle East face significant challenges in managing their workforce:

- **Compliance Complexity**: UAE labor laws require specific document management (Emirates ID, Labor Cards, Visas, Work Permits)
- **Manual Processes**: 70% of SMEs still use spreadsheets for HR management
- **Payroll Errors**: Manual payroll processing leads to 15-20% error rates
- **Attendance Tracking**: Traditional punch cards are prone to buddy punching and time theft
- **Scattered Data**: Employee information spread across multiple systems
- **Cost Barriers**: Enterprise HR systems (SAP, Oracle) cost $100K+ annually

### The Solution

A comprehensive, affordable, UAE-compliant HR Management System that automates all core HR functions while maintaining compliance with local regulations.
